Description

Welcome to GMRIT. Ranked 188 at National level in NIRF ranking, MHRD, New Delhi, July 2022. | Ranked 3rd in the state of AP and ranked a 8th among the “Outstanding Engineering colleges of Excellence” at the country level by CSR–GHRDC for the year 2022.

GMR Group is one of the fastest growing infrastructure enterprises in the country with interests in Airports, Energy, Highways and Urban Infrastructure Sectors. Employing the Public Private Partnership model, the Group has successfully implemented several iconic projects in India.

As a part of the GMRVF, GMRIT is an Autonomous Engineering College located in lush green campus of 158 acres in a place called Rajam, Srikakulam District (2 hrs. drive from Vizag). We have been accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ grade and NBA. We offer 9 UG and 6 PG programmes. We are also accredited by TCS, Infosys, WIPRO etc. and large companies like TCS, L&T, Mahindra Sathyam, and HCL Technologies recruit students from our institute regularly.

GMRIT offers its community a stimulating and enabling learning environment. The campus is, in every way, geared for shared knowledge and constant inquiry. Located far from the distractions of city living, spread over 117 acres and equipped with all major facilities, students and faculty can look forward to a fruitful and memorable learning experience at the Institute.

GMRIT offers the Indian student community competitive technical education, industry exposure and internationally-benchmarked teaching methodologies. With a mission to strive for the highest standards of excellence, GMRIT has in place, state-of-the-art infrastructure and a dedicated faculty, on an idyllic campus that is far removed from the distractions of city life.

Rapid globalisation and fast-changing technology necessitate leadership with vision. At GMRIT, students are moulded to face every challenge and sieze every opportunity that awaits them. The Institute is committed to inculcating in every student the confidence, integrity and ability to compete and excel in their chosen careers. I am certain, future employers will find GMRIT graduates among their most valuable assets in times to come.
